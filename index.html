We are a small yard based in beautiful Surrey offering full and occasional part livery for your horse or pony in need of either rest, recuperation and convalescing or retirement.

We cater for ridden horses also on a full or part livery basis.

We are committed to caring for your horses in a quiet relaxed manner to ensure their stay is safe peaceful and comfortable. With Twenty years experience working with and caring for top class competition horses we know that injured or elderly horses need expert care and individual attention to aid satisfactory recovery.

Being a small yard we offer a stress free environment. Horses on box rest can benefit from daily use of our Magnetic Medic Rug or leg wraps. For the horses that are able to enjoy regular turnout we have small individual paddocks that are well maintained all year round.

We look forward to caring for your horses be it short or long term.
