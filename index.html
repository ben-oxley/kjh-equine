Prices 

Livery and Services

    This Includes:
  • Basic hard feed twice daily,
  • Hay,
  • Bedding of either two bales of shavings per week or ad lib straw of top quality and made onsite,
  • Turnout and bring in,
  • Feet picked daily,
  • Rug changes and
  • Use of shared facilities.

Full livery £130 per week

(charged four weeks in advance) = £520

    As part livery with the following extras:
  • Horse exercised four times weekly.
  • Daily full groom.
  • Tack cleaning.
  • Horse held for Vet, Farrier and Chiropractor / Physiotherapist.
All horses will be regularly wormed and owners charged accordingly.

Extras

Extras include:
  • Clipping and trimming. Charges depending upon the type of clip required.
  • Trimming and show preparation.
  • If rugs need cleaning or repair they can be taken too and from a local cleaning facility.
  • Extra shavings will be charged at £7 per bale.

Care
with Experience

With more than twenty years experience working with and caring for top class competition horses in all disciplines. We are committed to caring for horses in a quiet and professional manner to ensure their stay is comfortable peaceful and safe. Unfortunately we cannot accommodate dogs or children under 16 years old as this is a private family home.

We also cater for horses in need of rest recuperation and convalescence due to injury. A short drive away is an Equine Hydrotherapy Spa. This can benefit the healing process and aid recovery.
